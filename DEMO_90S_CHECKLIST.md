# Demo 90s Checklist

## Goal

Hit the full story in under `90 seconds` with no dead time.

## Exact Order

1. Open `/about`.
2. Say: `OneRealm is a playable GameFi loop on OneChain testnet with gasless onboarding and on-chain owned assets.`
3. Open `/`.
4. Click `Enter Judge Mode`.
5. On `/hero`, mint one hero with any archetype and profession.
6. Say: `Identity matters because archetype and profession change mission bonus and crafting progression.`
7. Open that hero's inventory.
8. Click `Claim Bundle`.
9. Craft `Raider Blade`.
10. Equip the crafted weapon.
11. Say: `Rewards become materials, salvage, and profession-gated crafting instead of useless gear inflation.`
12. Open quest screen.
13. Choose `Expedition`.
14. Start the run.
15. Say: `Judge mode only shortens expedition time to 30 seconds. The real authority, ownership, and settlement flow stay on-chain.`
16. Wait for countdown to finish.
17. Resolve expedition.
18. Show new rewards and close with: `This is already live on OneChain testnet and fully playable end-to-end.`

## Fallbacks

- If mint flakes, use an existing hero and continue from inventory.
- If RPC feels stale, wait 2-3 seconds and retry once.
- If browser state gets messy, refresh and re-enter judge mode.
